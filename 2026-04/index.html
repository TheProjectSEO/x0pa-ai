Executive Summary
What matters, why it matters, what happens next.
  1. Google organic traffic tripled in 90 days. GSC clicks grew from 5,431 (Jan) to 18,204 (Mar), impressions from 1.64M to 5.15M, and average ranking improved from 14.9 to 7.8 — a meaningful move from page 2 to page 1 across the ranked keyword base.
  2. Rankings improved on every commercial keyword that matters. "AI recruitment software" moved from position 37 to 17. "AI hiring software" from 41 to 14. "AI recruitment" from 72 to 27. "AI screening" from 32 to position 3.6. Four new commercial keywords started ranking in February: AI sourcing, AI hiring platform, AI applicant tracking system, AI in recruiting — all entering at positions 29–48.
  3. AI search referrals are validated and growing. 1,429 sessions Jan–Mar from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot, and Claude combined (GA4 source/medium data). ChatGPT alone: 179 → 388 → 483 sessions per month. This surface is compounding as AI search adoption expands.
  4. Lead attribution is a known gap right now. X0PA migrated CRM from HubSpot to Freshsales. During migration, UTM capture was not wired on the 15 lead-capture forms — so 99.8% of new contacts currently land with source = "Unknown." Freshsales does show 348 new contacts in March + 21 deals in Q1 — volume is tracked, organic attribution is not yet. Fixing this is the single highest-leverage next action to close the full funnel loop.

April work: submit for indexing, add internal linking, optimize for target commercial keywords. Engineering dependency: deploy UTM parameter capture on the 15 lead-capture forms and map to cf_traffic_source in Freshsales. Once shipped, we close the full loop and the May report includes organic-attributed leads and deals for the first time.

With UTM capture deployed and feature pages indexed, we'll report: (1) organic-attributed contact and deal volume from Freshsales, (2) first-click and first-impression data for the new feature pages, (3) movement on commercial keyword rankings, and (4) continued Q2 trend on AI search referrals.
