★ Executive Summary
The story in five bullets, in the order a decision-maker should read it.
  1. X0PA is accelerating while competitors stall. Clicks +235%, impressions +213%, organic keywords +183% — all in Q1 2026. Meanwhile SmartRecruiters, Eightfold, and impress.ai shed a combined 6,559 keywords.
  2. Bottom-funnel traffic is growing +73% and converting clicks at nearly 2× the CTR of top-funnel content. The calculators-and-interview-questions strategy is feeding commercial page visibility exactly as designed.
  3. Average GSC position moved from 14.9 to 7.8 in 90 days — a meaningful step from page 2 to page 1 on a volume-weighted basis.
  4. AEO is driving real sessions. 385 AI citations (up 20× year over year) are converting into 487 direct ChatGPT referral sessions. Real users, not vanity metrics. This advantage compounds as AI search expands.
  5. Lead attribution remains the open gap. Freshsales shows 348 new contacts in March + 21 deals in Q1, but 99.8% of contacts still land with source = "Unknown" because UTM capture is not yet deployed on the 15 lead-capture forms.

13.18% dead-click rate is fixable. This ties back to the INP 288ms finding: slow interactions make users click repeatedly on elements that are already responding (just slowly), which Clarity registers as "dead clicks." Fixing INP addresses both metrics simultaneously.

Clarity also logs form-interaction events (13,605) and "book" clicks (1,011), but these fire on any form submission — contact forms, calculator inputs, newsletter signups, filters — so they are engagement signals, not validated leads. Verified lead volume is tracked in Freshsales: 348 new contacts in March and 21 deals opened in Q1 2026.

→ Priorities for April & May
Four actions ordered by expected impact.
  1. Core Web Vitals field data. LCP 3.24s and INP 288ms both need reduction. Focus on the homepage and calculator templates — these drive ~70% of all sessions. Target: LCP ≤ 2.5s, INP ≤ 200ms within 60 days.
  2. Commercial keyword push. "ai recruitment software," "ai hiring platform," "ai talent acquisition" sit at positions 30–60 with 15,000+ combined monthly impressions. Pushing one or two into page 1 adds material commercial traffic directly correlated with demo requests.
  3. UX cleanup from Clarity. 13.18% dead-click rate is fixable. Audit the dead-click heatmaps and resolve the top 5 offenders. Also addresses rage-click pattern.
  4. UTM capture deployment. Unblocks full organic-to-lead attribution for the May report. Single highest-leverage engineering change to close the organic → contact → deal loop.
What changes in the May report. With UTM capture deployed, we'll be able to report organic-attributed contacts and organic-attributed deal volume for the first time — turning the current "traffic and rankings" narrative into a full funnel measurement from impression to revenue.
